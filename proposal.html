Project Proposal: Spelling Adventures

Problem Domain

In the realm of parenting, a pressing challenge often faced by new parents is the availability of high-quality and accessible educational resources for their young children. This issue relates to the broader domain of early childhood education, where vocabulary development plays a pivotal role. Research demonstrates the critical connection between a child's vocabulary growth and their overall school achievement [1]. Moreover, a child's vocabulary size in kindergarten serves as a strong predictor of their ability to learn to read [2], highlighting the profound impact of early language skills on educational success. The more words a child knows, the more information they can access, fostering critical thinking and a deeper understanding of the world around them. Encouraging children's vocabulary development is essential to ensure they acquire the language and literacy skills necessary to thrive in school and beyond. However, despite the importance of this issue, there is an information technology problem that needs attention and innovation. Existing websites and apps attempt to address the vocabulary development challenge but often fall short in terms of content quality, accessibility, or user experience. To bridge this gap effectively, a comprehensive web application is needed to cater to the vocabulary development needs of both parents and young learners. Our proposed word teaching website aims to address this problem by providing a valuable tool for parents to facilitate the education of their young children. Through our platform, parents can access a wealth of educational materials and interesting activities designed to support their child's early learning journey, making the process of nurturing and educating young minds more manageable and enjoyable.
